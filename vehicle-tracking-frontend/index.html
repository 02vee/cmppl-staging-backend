About Alccofine

Alccofine is a pioneering company specializing in micro fine mineral additives technology. As a joint venture between Ambuja Cements Ltd and the Alcon group Goa, Alccofine offers high-performance durable concrete solutions.

Their products, such as the Alccofine Series 1200 and Series 1100, are widely used in enhancing concrete performance and in applications like soil stabilization and tunnel grouting.
